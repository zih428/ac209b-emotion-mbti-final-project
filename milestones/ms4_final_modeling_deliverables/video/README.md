# MS4 Video Workspace

Use this folder for the six-minute video script, timing plan, recording checklist, and final video link notes.

Timing budget suggestion:

| Segment | Time |
|---|---:|
| Team intro and question | 0:30 |
| Data and key EDA | 1:00 |
| MS3 baseline diagnosis | 1:00 |
| Final modeling design: corrected baselines, transformer author features, and emotion controls | 1:30 |
| Results and interpretation | 1:30 |
| Conclusion and limitations | 0:30 |

Every team member should have a real speaking role.

The video should describe emotion probabilities as transferred text-derived representations. Avoid causal wording such as "emotion causes MBTI prediction"; the stronger claim is whether real emotion representations add incremental author-level predictive information beyond matched text models and shuffled-emotion controls.
