- Submissions were due at 9:59pm on the deadline day.
- Late days did not apply to project submissions.

## Purpose

Students proposed a project aligned with personal, professional, or academic interests. The proposal should be concise and focused on an available public dataset and a clear data science problem.

## Proposal Checklist

- Author name and email.
- Data link and brief overview.
- Background and motivation.
- Problem statement.
- Scope and methods.
- Concerns and limitations.
