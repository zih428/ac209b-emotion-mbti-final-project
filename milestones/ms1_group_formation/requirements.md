## Dates

- Staff released approved projects: Tuesday, March 3, 2026.
- Team formation and project preferences due: Tuesday, March 24, 2026.
- Projects assigned: Friday, March 27, 2026.

## Purpose

This milestone formed project teams and collected ranked project preferences.

## Requirements

- Groups should consist of 3-5 members.
- Team members should join one premade project group on Canvas.
- One group member submitted the group's ranked preferences.
- Groups ranked their top four project choices.
- If a student could not find a group, they could submit preferences individually and staff would try to place them.
